1. Introduction

Welcome to Répondly. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you interact with our AI-powered automation services through WhatsApp Cloud API, Facebook, and Instagram.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our services, we may collect the following information:

  • ▸ Phone Numbers: Your WhatsApp phone number is collected when you initiate a conversation with our automated system via the WhatsApp Cloud API.
  • ▸ Message History: We store the content of messages exchanged between you and our AI assistant to provide context-aware responses and improve service quality.
  • ▸ Facebook & Instagram Data: When interacting through Facebook Messenger or Instagram DMs, we collect your profile name, user ID, and message content as permitted by Meta's Platform Policies.
  • ▸ Language & Query Data: Queries submitted in Darija, French, or Arabic are processed to deliver accurate, localized responses.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • ▸ To provide automated customer support and AI-driven responses on behalf of our clients.
  • ▸ To process and understand your queries using OpenAI's language models (GPT), which analyze message content to generate relevant replies.
  • ▸ To maintain conversation context and history for a seamless user experience.
  • ▸ To comply with legal obligations and Meta's Platform Terms of Service.

4. AI Processing (OpenAI)

Our platform uses OpenAI's API to process customer queries. This means message content may be transmitted to OpenAI's servers for inference. OpenAI does not use API data to train their models by default. We process queries in multiple languages including Darija (Moroccan Arabic), French, and Modern Standard Arabic.

For more information on how OpenAI handles data, please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

5. Meta Platform Compliance

Répondly operates in full compliance with Meta's Platform Policies for WhatsApp Cloud API, Facebook, and Instagram. Specifically:

  • ▸ We only message users who have opted in or initiated contact with our services.
  • ▸ We do not use collected data for advertising or sell it to third parties.
  • ▸ Message data obtained via WhatsApp Cloud API is used solely for delivering the requested service.
  • ▸ We comply with Meta's requirements regarding data retention, user consent, and prohibited use cases.

6. Data Storage & Security

All data collected by Répondly is stored securely on our private servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • ▸ Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.
  • ▸ Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only.
  • ▸ Regular security audits and monitoring.
  • ▸ Data is not shared with third parties except as required to deliver the service (e.g., OpenAI API calls).

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. Conversation history is retained for a maximum of 12 months unless a deletion request is submitted earlier.
